Danfoss Link
intelligent provider of a better living

With more than 80 years of experience, innovation and development, Danfoss is now offering you the best solutions which provide perfect comfort while reducing your heating costs. Nowadays life can be unpredictable and Danfoss Link offers you flexible solution that gives you control of the heating with a single touch, in each room of the house, from wherever you are.

Our satisfaction rises with our happy costumers. Bettina and Thomas (Germany) moved to their house ten years ago, and because the house was 40 years old it needed to be modernized. After an unsuccessful investment in the central heating system, they fit the homes 10 radiators with Danfoss living connect* thermostats and tie them wirelessly together in a Danfoss Link™ system. Thomas said: “Now I can always rely on the temperature to be right. If I want 21˚C, I’ll get it. At the same time, the Danfoss Link system has helped us reduce our energy consumption considerably.” To make it even easier for our customers, Danfoss Link offers an app which can be connected to Danfoss Link™ Central Controller. The app is simple, elegant and easy to use. It enables to easily adjust your homes heating system or get an overview from wherever you are.
